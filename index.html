About Me

Hi, I’m Arra (short for Arnara Livius Elsyifa). I started building games in Scratch, discovering how logic and creativity click together like puzzle pieces. From there I jumped into Unity to explore 2D/3D worlds and scripting with C#, and I also experiment in Roblox Studio to prototype multiplayer experiences quickly.

I love designing interactive, creative experiences that are polished, accessible, and fun. My goal is to keep learning, ship small projects, and grow into a professional game developer.
